# Adidas Sales Dashboard

An interactive Business Intelligence dashboard designed to analyze and visualize Adidas sales performance across regions, product categories, and key timeline metrics.

---

## Dashboard Overview
<img width="1324" height="637" alt="Capture" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7b0dc5ec-7464-4833-a8a5-af07743db418" />


The **Adidas Sales Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of critical business metrics to assist stakeholders in tracking commercial performance, understanding product demand, and monitoring regional sales distribution. 

### Key High-Level Metrics (KPIs)
* **Total Sales:** 287,378
* **Total Orders:** 1,200
* **Units Sold:** 3,550

### Visualizations Breakdown
* **Order & Sales by Category:** Breakdown of Footwear, Apparel, and Accessories, highlighting Footwear as the leading driver in both total volume and sales revenue.
* **Top 10 Products:** A ranked bar chart of top-performing items by units sold, led by classic models like the *Stan Smith* (246 units) and *Superstar* (220 units).
* **Monthly Order Trends:** A line graph charting monthly seasonal volume, showing peak activity in May (127 orders) and a downward trajectory toward November.
* **Regional Sales Distribution:** An area chart mapping revenue distribution across key Middle Eastern markets, led significantly by Egypt and Iraq.

---

## Features

* **Multi-Dimensional Filtering:** Slice data dynamically by **Category** (Accessories, Apparel, Footwear), **Region** (Egypt, Iraq, KSA, Lebanon, Oman), and **Order Date** (Yearly timeline slider for 2023–2025).
* **Dark Mode Visuals:** High-contrast dark theme optimized for modern desktop presentations and executive reporting.
* **Scannable Layout:** Left-to-right structured workflow separating categorical breakdowns, top products, time-series trends, and regional performance.

---

## How to Use

1.  **Filter by Category:** Click on the "Category" slicer to isolate metrics specifically for Footwear, Apparel, or Accessories.
2.  **Filter by Region:** Select one or multiple countries from the "Region" pane to view isolated geographical datasets.
3.  **Adjust Timeline:** Use the timeline slider at the bottom right to filter the dataset between fiscal years 2023, 2024, and 2025.
